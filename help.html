Guide and Help

Everything you need to create a working imagemap for your osu! profile.

Step 0 - Host your image

osu! BBCode requires a direct image URL, a link that ends with an image extension like .png or .jpg and loads the raw image with no webpage around it. You need to upload your image to a hosting service first.

What is a direct image URL?
A direct URL points straight to the image file.
It usually looks like https://files.catbox.moe/46ffuo.jpg.
important: image must be less than 4MB to work properly
How to use the tool
1
Paste your direct image URL
In the sidebar, find the Image section. Paste your direct image URL into the input field and click Load Image. Your image will appear on the canvas.
2
Add a clickable box
Click + Add Box. A pink-outlined box will appear in the top-left corner of your image. You can add as many boxes as you need, one per clickable area.
3
Drag and resize the box
Click and drag the box to move it over the area you want to be clickable. Drag any of the 8 edge and corner handles to resize it precisely. The box will stay within the image boundary.
4
Set the link and hover text
With a box selected, the Selected Box panel appears in the sidebar.

Link URL is where the box links to when clicked, like your osu! profile.
Hover Text is the tooltip shown when a user hovers over the area.

Example link: https://osu.ppy.sh/users/your_user_id
5
Repeat for all areas
Add more boxes for any other clickable regions, like social links, collab entries, or friend profiles. Each box is independent. Use the box list in the sidebar to select or delete them.
6
Copy the BBCode
When you're done, click Copy BBCode. The generated code is now in your clipboard, ready to paste into your osu! profile.
[imagemap] https://files.catbox.moe/46ffuo.jpg 5.00 10.00 40.00 25.00 https://osu.ppy.sh/users/123456 my profile [/imagemap]
Format: each box line is x y width height url hovertext, all as percentages of the image size. You do not need to edit this manually; the tool generates it for you.
Putting it in your osu! profile
1
Go to your profile
Open osu.ppy.sh and go to your profile page. Click the pencil / edit icon next to your username or bio section.
2
Open the "me!" bio section
Scroll down to the "me!" tab on your profile page (unlocked the first time you get osu! supporter) Click the pencil icon to enter edit mode.
3
Paste your BBCode
In the text editor, paste the [imagemap]...[/imagemap] code you copied from this tool. You can combine it with other BBCode like [centre] to centre it on the page.
[centre] [imagemap] https://files.catbox.moe/46ffuo.jpg 5.00 10.00 40.00 25.00 https://osu.ppy.sh/users/34263657 my profile [/imagemap] [/centre]
4
Save and preview
Click Save. Visit your profile to see the imagemap live. Hover over your boxes to check the tooltips, and click them to confirm the links work. If something looks off, go back to the tool, adjust the boxes, and copy the updated BBCode.
Common questions
My image won't load in the tool.
Make sure you're using a direct image URL. The link should end in .png, .jpg, or similar, and open as a raw image when pasted into your browser.
